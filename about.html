About Share & Serve

Built to connect surplus with service

We help donors, NGOs, and volunteers coordinate faster so perfectly good meals can reach people in need instead of being wasted.

Our Mission

Share & Serve connects surplus food donors with NGO partners to reduce waste and support hunger relief in local communities.

How It Works

Restaurants, events, hotels, and households can list extra food. NGOs browse available donations, claim pickups, and coordinate distribution.

Why It Matters

Every timely pickup saves food, reduces landfill waste, and helps deliver nutritious meals to people who need support the most.
